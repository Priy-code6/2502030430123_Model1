✦ Our Story

Born from Passion,
Refined by Craft

A Legacy of Elegance

Founded with a passion for timeless elegance and modern tradition, Noor Vastra began as the singular dream of Priyanka Kadam — a fashion-obsessed visionary who believed that luxury shouldn't be a privilege, but a feeling accessible to those who dare to define their own standard of beauty.

What began as a dream to bring graceful, luxurious designs to every wardrobe has grown into a fashion destination loved for its sophistication, craftsmanship, and individuality.

Our collections draw from a dialogue between Indian heritage and global contemporary design — rich textures meet clean silhouettes, vivid hues honour restraint, and every stitch carries the intention of artisans who treat their craft as sacred.

We believe a great outfit isn't just what you wear. It's what you carry with you — confidence, identity, and the silent authority of someone who knows exactly who they are.

Priyanka Kadam
Founder, Creative Director & CEO

Priyanka Kadam is the singular creative force and founder behind Noor Vastra.She built this brand from the ground up — turning a personal obsession with elevated Indian fashion into a movement that now reaches over 120,000 customers across the country. Every collection, every stitch, every campaign reflects her singular vision: that luxury must feel both timeless and deeply personal.
